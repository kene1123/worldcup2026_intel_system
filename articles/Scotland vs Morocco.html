Expect a fascinating tactical battle that pits contrasting styles against each other. Scotland, under their astute management, will likely set up in a disciplined and organized manner, probably a compact 3-4-2-1 or 4-3-3 formation. Their strength lies in their collective spirit, physical presence in midfield, and an unwavering commitment to the cause. Morocco, on the other hand, will aim to dictate the tempo, employing a dynamic 4-2-3-1 or 4-3-3 system designed to unleash their technical prowess. Expect them to try and stretch Scotland, creating spaces in behind and around the box for their more creative talents to exploit.

The key battles will be numerous: Scotland's physical midfield against Morocco's agile playmakers; Morocco's quick wingers against Scotland's resilient full-backs; and the duel for control of the second balls.
